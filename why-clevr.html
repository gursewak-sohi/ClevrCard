Why Clevr

Using ClevrCard for all your banking needs offers a range of benefits and conveniences that can greatly enhance your financial management

  • Streamlined Wallet

    Streamline your wallet and eliminate the need to carry multiple cards.

  • Zero interest

    When your savings account balance is above your Clevr card balance, you pay zero interest on your purchases. It's that simple.

  • Flexibility and Control

    Choose how you want to pay. Use your credit line for larger purchases, while accessing funds directly from your checking account for day-to-day expenses.

  • Convenience and Accessibility

    Whether you need to make a purchase, withdraw cash, transfer funds, or monitor your accounts, we provide easy and secure access.

  • Strong Security Measures

    We implement robust security measures to ensure your accounts and transactions are protected.

  • Competitive Interest Rates

    We provide a dedicated space for you to save towards your goals, ensuring that your savings grow over time.
