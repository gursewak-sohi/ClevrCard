Savings Account

The ClevrCard Savings Account is a secure and convenient way to save money while enjoying competitive interest rates. Opening a Savings Account with CleverCard provides you with a dedicated space to set aside funds and work towards your financial goals.

Effectively manage and grow your savings

Our credit card limit scales with you

Competitive Interest Rates

ClevrCard offers competitive interest rates on your savings, allowing your money to grow over time.

No Minimum Deposit Requirement

You can open a CleverCard Savings Account with no minimum deposit, making it accessible for savers at all levels.

Flexibility and Accessibility

Access your CleverCard Savings Account conveniently through online banking, mobile apps, and other channels, making it easy to deposit, withdraw, and monitor your savings at your convenience.

FDIC Insurance

Your CleverCard Savings Account is FDIC insured, which means your funds are protected up to the maximum limit allowed by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation.

Seamless Integration

  • Your ClevrCard Savings Account integrates seamlessly with your ClevrCard credit card and checking account, providing a comprehensive financial solution.

  • Transfer funds effortlessly: Move money between your ClevrCard Savings Account and ClevrCard Checking Account to manage your finances efficiently and make the most of your savings

Saving Made Simple

  • With ClevrCard, saving money becomes easier and more rewarding. You can automate regular deposits into your Savings Account or make manual transfers to help you stay consistent with your savings goals.

  • Whether you're saving for emergencies, future expenses, or long-term goals, ClevrCard's Savings Account empowers you to take control of your financial future.
